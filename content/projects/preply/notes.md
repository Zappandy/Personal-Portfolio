## What I built

Full backend for an AI-powered teaching assistant at the Preply × Agora hackathon. Responsible for agent orchestration, LLM routing logic, and API integration across multiple third-party services. The system routes student queries to the appropriate specialist agent depending on subject and context.

## What I learned

How to efficiently use Open-AI APIs instead of open-source solutions for a customer-facing shystem. While I prefer open-source and more specialized models, it was really cool experience to learn how huge models from the big players in the field are being used!
