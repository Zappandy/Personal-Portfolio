About

Engineer. Researcher. Builder.

I'm a Scientific ML Engineer with a research background in NLP and scientific AI. My work lives at the intersection of large-scale systems engineering and fundamental research — I care about building things that work at scale and understanding why they work.

I've trained and fine-tuned large language models on MareNostrum5 (one of Europe's fastest supercomputers), reduced climate data pipelines from hours to minutes, and published at ACL — top-tier NLP research.
